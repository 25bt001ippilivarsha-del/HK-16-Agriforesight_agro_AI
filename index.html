🌾 Agro AI - User Guide

Complete guide to reduce post-harvest losses and maximize your farming profits

About This System

Agro AI is an intelligent farming assistant that helps reduce post-harvest losses by up to 40% through real-time climate analysis, smart storage recommendations, and market intelligence.

  • AI-powered crop recommendations based on your location
  • Real-time weather monitoring with spoilage prediction
  • Market price tracking with best selling time advice
  • Multilingual support for 10 Indian languages

Dashboard

Your command center showing real-time weather, spoilage risk, recommended crops, best markets, and climate signals all in one view.

1 Allow location access for accurate local data
2 View live weather and humidity conditions
3 Check yield forecasts and risk distribution charts

Spoilage Risk Prediction

Predict how long your harvest will last and get storage optimization tips to minimize losses.

1 Select your crop type (Rice, Wheat, Maize, etc.)
2 Enter harvest date and storage conditions
3 Adjust temperature and humidity sliders
4 Get AI prediction with sell-by date and storage tips

Crop Planning

Get smart crop recommendations based on your soil, season, water availability, and market demand.

1 Enter your farm area in acres
2 Select irrigation type and soil type
3 Choose risk preference and planning horizon
4 View crop rotation plan and soil regeneration tips

Market Intelligence

Track prices, predict demand, and find the best time and place to sell your harvest.

1 View real-time price trends for different crops
2 Enter your crop quantity and transport costs
3 Compare local vs nearby market profits
4 Get 4-week price forecast and optimal selling window

AI Farming Assistant

Ask any farming question in your language! Voice input supported for easy use.

1 Click the Help button (bottom right corner)
2 Use quick question buttons or type your question
3 Tap microphone to ask by voice in your language
4 Get AI-powered answers with local context
